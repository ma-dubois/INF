TitreCours: CO-ADMI 810J Organizational Behavior and Theory
DescriptionCours: To provide a detailed overview of the field of Organizational Behavior/Theory (OB/T). It will cover the basic concepts, theory and research of the field, and focus on all levels of analysis ranging from individual to organizational and interorganizational behavior. Both foundational and contemporary leading edge material will be addressed. The design assumes a wide audience and aims at providing a strong OB/T base for students specializing in this area, and providing solid background knowledge in behavioral issues in organizations for students specializing in other fields (e.g., Strategy, Marketing, Finance, etc.).  &nbsp;  &nbsp; Pour consulter l'horaire des cours, veuillez cliquer ici &nbsp;
