TitreCours: Portfolio Management
DescriptionCours: (This course aims to teach and apply the concepts introduced in 2-201-99 introduction to investments and 3-210-99 Options and futures. The objective of the course is to introduce the students to the different aspects of portfolio management, specifically to isssues related to assets management (strategic, tactic and dynamic), and to investment strategies of portfolios that include stocks, fixed income securities and derivatives. Some of the subjects that are covered include, among others, the process of portfolio management, portfolio theories, hedging porfolios, investment styles (value or growth), portfolio indexation, immunization. Asset valuation and portfolio composition (models by Goldman Sachs, BARRA, Wilshire, etc.) are also examined. A team simulation of a portfolio of Canadian securities is a requirement of the course.&nbsp;&ndash;&nbsp;Translation not available.)
