TitreCours: Management Skills
DescriptionCours: To sensitise students to the importance of skills (as opposed to talent) in the exercise of authority  To begin a life-long learning process about the development of such skills  &nbsp;  The course touches on key topics such as learning, teamwork, communication, negotiating, handling conflict, politics, power and authority. Very practical in orientation, it is offered in the initial stages of the program in order to be of immediate use both during your studies as well as immediately afterward on the job market.  &nbsp;
