TitreCours: Operations Management
DescriptionCours: General Overview of the course  This course presents the basic concepts of the strategic and operational management of Operations as well as their application in the manufacturing sector.&nbsp; However, applications in the service sector will be briefly presented.&nbsp; Emphasis will be on the ways and means to optimize the perfomance of the organization through the effective design and running of its Operations system.&nbsp; The means are part of the company's general strategy.  The course briefly presents relatively new concepts such as trends in management, cross-functional Management, Stakehorlders' Management, Supply Chain Management &amp; Logistics, Enterprise Resources Planning (ERP), Mass Customization, Concurrent Engineering, Just-in-Time, Skill-Based Pay, Learning Organization, and ISA 9000 Standards Series, Suppliers Relationship Management and Customer Relationship Management.
