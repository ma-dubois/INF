TitreCours: Management Service Operations
DescriptionCours: Because services are intangible and because the customer is often an intrinsic part of service operations, the performance of service organizations relies on their ability to design and manage complex processes.&nbsp; The main goals of this course are:  To better understand the characteristics, stakes and problems that are specific to service processes;  To illustrate how service operations are intertwined with marketing,&nbsp; personnel management and business strategy;  To provide some basic conceptual and technical tools that can be used to analyze and improve service processes;  To explore opportunities for innovation and growth in particular service sectors and to better anticipate the impacts of new technologies.
