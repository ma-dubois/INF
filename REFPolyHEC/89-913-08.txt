TitreCours: CO-ADMI 860G Accounting Research: Financial Reporting and Capital Markets
DescriptionCours: This advanced seminar will provide a critical forum for the in-depth review and discussion of contemporary accounting research as it relates to financial reporting and capital markets. Through conscientious analysis of original journal articles from leading accounting and finance journals (rather than literature review articles), students are expected to:   &nbsp;develop a comprehensive understanding of contemporary issues in market-based accounting research  be able to critically evaluate empirical financial accounting research and;  obtain methodological perspectives by assessing the myriad choices of research methods made by leading accounting researchers.  &nbsp;  &nbsp;&nbsp; To Consult Seminars'Schedule, Click Here &nbsp;
