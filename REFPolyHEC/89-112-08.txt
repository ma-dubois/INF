TitreCours: CO-ADMI 831L Advanced Topics in Marketing
DescriptionCours: The course objectives are: (1) to introduce the students to a set of advanced substantive areas in marketing, (2) to encourage students to develop meaningful and substantiated criticisms of assigned readings, (3) to provide students the framework to identify and develop their research areas, (4) to help students develop fundamental understanding of different substantive areas to increase communication and collaboration with other areas of marketing. Given these goals, the course will cover a wide range of topics and introduce students to a selected number of influential papers in each area. This course is designed primarily for the first and second year PhD students.
