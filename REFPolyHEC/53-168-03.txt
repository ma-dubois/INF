TitreCours: Managing Products and Brands
DescriptionCours: This course exposes the main issues pertaining to brand and product management from a strategic, methodological and ethical perspective. The students will be guided into comprehending the essential aspects of the establishment and the development of brands on national as well as international markets, and to acquire a sufficient knowledge of brand management, allowing them to follow its evolution and to translate this updated knowledge into efficient managerial decisions.
