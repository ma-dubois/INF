TitreCours: Portfolio Management
DescriptionCours: This is the capstone course in financial management. It follows Analysis Fixed Income Securities (53-262-02) and Valuation of Common Stocks (53-263-02). Whereas the analysis of each type of security was the focus of each pre-requisite course, the main objective of Portfolio Management is to integrate the knowledge in the two previous courses. This is achieved by examining the adequate mix of investment assets and its management. Specifically, the course emphasizes the importance of the process of portfolio management, assett allocation decision, optimal relationship between risk and return, and evaluation of portfolio performance.
