TitreCours: Corporate Finance II
DescriptionCours: This is the second part of the two MBA core courses in corporate finance. The first course&nbsp;Corporate Finance I introduced the effect of the time value of money and the discounting techniques necessary to analyze and implement optimal investment decisions by firms.  &nbsp;  In Corporate Finance II we study the effect of uncertainty in the cash flows forecasts,&nbsp; and the discount rate&nbsp;on decision making. Then we discuss the financing decision of the firm and finally put the pieces together.  &nbsp;  Topics include the risk and return trade-off, diversification, estimation of cost of debt and equity, weighted average cost of capital, the main theories on capital structure, dividend policy, and risk management.
