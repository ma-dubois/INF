TitreCours: QM-ADM 993Y Issues in Management Thinking
DescriptionCours: This seminar offers an in-depth examination and ontological analysis of key issues in management thinking and practice.The seminar aims at providing students with an comprehensive and interdisciplinary perspective on the analysis and management og conflict in the development. The course examines the nature and forms of conflict in the course of developement management as well as causes, processes, and management of conflict at the individual, group, and organizational levels. The courses draws on several disciplinary systems, including traditional management thinking, systems thinking, depth psychology, economics, mythology, political science, cultural and religious perspectives, and anthropology.&nbsp;  &nbsp;  &nbsp; Pour consulter l'horaire des cours, veuillez cliquer ici &nbsp;&nbsp;
