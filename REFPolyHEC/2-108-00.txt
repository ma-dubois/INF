TitreCours: Marketing Management
DescriptionCours: This marketing course is intended to further your understanding of marketing as a function, a set of processes, and a business approach. Often, marketing (people) is (are) considered to be responsible for identifying customer needs and finding ways to fulfill these needs. However, the role of marketing (people) may also encompass a number of other processes - and organizations are also well advised not to leave them solely up to marketing (people). In this respect, this course will outline how organizations thrive through exchange processes with various constituencies and how marketing thought, marketing techniques, and marketing processes can foster such exchange processes in a way to reach the company's objectives.
