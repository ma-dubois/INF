TitreCours: Financial Analysis
DescriptionCours: The main objective of this course is to enable the student to master the analysis and interpretation of financial statements.  &nbsp;  The DuPont method of financial analysis is reviewed. Special attention is given to the analysis of commercial and financial risk. The limits of traditional financial analysis and the notion of quality of earnings are then presented. The analysis of the statement of cash flows is then reviewed. Finally, various applications of financial analysis are examined, such as the reorganisation of distressed businesses, and the forecasting of earnings per share for investment purposes.
