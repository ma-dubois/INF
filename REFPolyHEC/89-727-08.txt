TitreCours: CO-ADMI 822H Strategic Management of IT
DescriptionCours: This course covers the strategic management of information technology and investigates the potential use of information technology to improve organizational competitive advantage. Students are exposed to topics such as strategic alignment of information technology, business value of IT, IT governance, IT sourcing, management of the IT function, IS project management. The articles are mainly drawn from the top IS journals such as MIS Quarterly, Information Systems Research, Management Science, and Journal of Management Information Systems.
