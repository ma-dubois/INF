TitreCours: The Accounting Language
DescriptionCours: Managers are constantly expected to make decisions based on information provided by various sources. One of the most important of these sources is the accounting system that produces the financial statements. These statements are prepared according to a standardized set of rules that can be difficult to understand for the untrained eye. This course is designed to give the students a knowledge of the most important rules and standards used in financial reporting and a critical view on some controversial issues that can arise in financial statements of companies. attr attr
