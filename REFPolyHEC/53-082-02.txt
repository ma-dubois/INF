TitreCours: Management of Transportation Systems
DescriptionCours: The course "Management of Transportation Systems" introduces practical and theoretical concepts related to the transportation of energy. Emphasis will be on the pricing issues. The economics of the oil and the gas transportation will be discussed within the larger framework of energy projects developments. Practical and proposed models for pricing gas and electricity transportation in domestic markets will be discussed.  &nbsp;  Evaluation  Homeworks (60%) (3 at 20% each) Class participation 10% Final project: 30%
