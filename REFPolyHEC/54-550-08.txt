TitreCours: Sustainable Supply Chain
DescriptionCours: Sustainable Supply Chain is a course that covers a variety of managerial tools and concepts such as stakeholder analysis, breakeven calculations, risk management, operations strategy, supply management, and logistics.&nbsp; The course relies heavily on case studies and class discussion to foster students' knowledge: it is therefore imperative for each student to read the assigned material and to prepare thoroughly each case before class.&nbsp; In a nutshell the objectives are:  &nbsp;   To understand the implications of different governmental interventions on corporate and operations strategy.  To become knowledgeable of different strategies related to environmental management.  To develop a systemic and holistic view of environmental management (e.g., cross-functional and supply chain).  To examine different tools and techniques that leverage expertise in the supply chain through an enhanced environmental and social management.
