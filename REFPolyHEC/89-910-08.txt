TitreCours: CO-ADMI 860E Accounting Research : An Overview
DescriptionCours: As a professional field, accounting encompasses a wide range of activities, practices, and concepts with accounting professionals acting in various capacities. Hence, in their efforts to understand how and why accountants make decisions as well as the impact of these decisions, accounting researchers need to rely on a broad set of theoretical and methodological tools that are drawn from various disciplines. The purpose of the seminar is to expose students to these theoretical and methodological tools. However, by emphasizing emerging issues and current trends, the seminar adopts a forward-looking stance with respect to accounting themes research so that students can better identify relevant and promising research.  &nbsp;  &nbsp; Pour consulter l'horaire des cours, veuillez cliquer ici &nbsp;
