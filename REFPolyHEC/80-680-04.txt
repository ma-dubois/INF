TitreCours: Dynamic Optimisation in Management
DescriptionCours: The objective of this course is to introduce students to optimization models in a dynamic contest; more specifically, the students will be acquainted with the major tools used in dynamic optimization, that is, dynamic programming, markov decision problems and optimal control. In addition, the course will cover many applications where the use of such tools is called-for. At the end of the course, the students should be able to:  &nbsp;  -&nbsp;identify a business management situation presenting a dynamic optimization problem,&nbsp; -&nbsp;represent this problem under an suitable mathematical model,&nbsp; -&nbsp;and solve it using one of the available techniques of dynamic optimization.
