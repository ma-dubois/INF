TitreCours: Financial Markets
DescriptionCours: This course is an initiation to the Canadian financial system and its different markets.&nbsp; We shall talk about financial intermediation and securities brokerage.&nbsp; The economic and financial principles will be talked about.  The Canadian financial markets operation, and by the way, the USA financial markets are discussed.&nbsp; We shall discuss about savings and cash flows, intermediation and financial institutions, real and financial assets,inflation and yield rates, different markets and their characteristics, different types of orders, brokerage services, securities exchanged, mutual funds and ratings reading.&nbsp; This course also explain briefly public savings offerings, initial public offerings, rated securities new issues, the underwriting process, bonds and term structure of interest rates and also management, structure and bank loans market.&nbsp;
