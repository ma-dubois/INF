TitreCours: Structure of English for ESL teachers I
DescriptionCours: This course introduces future ESL teachers to a number of important aspects of English structure with the aim of preparing them to apply their knowledge to the teaching of ESL. Topics discussed include: phonology and phonetics: the teaching of pronunciation and corrective techniques; vocabulary: words structures, teaching meaning through vocabulary; pragmatics: the teaching of English for every-day use; English language varieties and standard usage; comparisons with the structure of French.
