TitreCours: Trading in Financial Markets
DescriptionCours: This course aims at introducing the students to the daily activities of a real trading room. The student is introduced to financial information platforms such as Reuters and Bloomberg, the wealth of information available through these platforms, and the analysis of the principal markets (equity, fixed income, currency, and commodities).  In addition to this the student is introduced to how trading may be conducted, the various types of trades, the role of the different market participants, and the importance of and the practical consequences of the organization of the financial markets.
