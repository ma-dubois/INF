TitreCours: CO-ADMI 851Q Seminar in Cross-Cultural Management
DescriptionCours: This course uses a dialectic approach to examine and contrast the debates within Strategic Management.&nbsp; Readings are included from both established and emerging debates, engaging in both retrospective and forward views of strategy.
