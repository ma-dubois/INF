TitreCours: CO-ADMI 831Q New Product Innovation and Marketing
DescriptionCours: This seminar provides a cross-section of classical and recent analytical literature on topics related to the development and marketing of new products and services. The topics covered in the seminar range from relatively broad, often cross-disciplinary, concepts such as Strategy and Diffusion, to narrower issues related to individual stages of new product development/marketing. The course attempts to cover the topic of new product innovation/marketing from both the consumer and industrial marketing viewpoint.  &nbsp;  &nbsp; Pour consulter l'horaire des cours, veuillez cliquer ici &nbsp;
