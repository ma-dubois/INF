TitreCours: IT Services Management
DescriptionCours: The students will acquire the knowledge and skills required to:  &nbsp;    Describe the disciplines, processes, roles and functions an IT organization needs to deliver quality IT services to the enterprise   State the key objectives of each discipline   Explain the advantages of an integrated approach to IT service delivery and support   Take the EXIN exam with confidence by applying their new-found&nbsp; knowledge   Put the ITIL (IT Infrastructure Library)&nbsp;principles to practical use.  &nbsp;  Course material is presented through lectures and exercises.
