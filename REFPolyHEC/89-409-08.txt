TitreCours: CO-ADMI 811R Current Research on Motivation and Self-Regulation in Organizations
DescriptionCours: This course reviews critical elements that influence worker motivation, including job design, management and compensation. Major motivation theories will be reviewed and evaluated. Current compensation and performance appraisal systems will be assessed.
