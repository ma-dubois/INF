TitreCours: Risk Analysis
DescriptionCours: The objective of this course is to present the quantitative analysis tools used to aid to tactic and strategic decision making under uncertainty in the energy sector. More specifically, the students will become familiar with the modelling of decision problem using different decision criteria and utility functions, scenario analysis, Monte-Carlo simulation and decision trees. Specialised software for the solution of specific problems will also be presented. Analysis and interpretation of results will be given as much emphasis as technical aspects.
