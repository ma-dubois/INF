TitreCours: Small Business Management
DescriptionCours: Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are acknowledged for their economic vitality, but generally speaking little is known about them.&nbsp;This course examines the world of SMEs in all its diversity.&nbsp;SME management is addressed through the administrative processes and strategic behaviours of the firms. The major challenges facing SMEs are discussed, with special focus on the context of globalization.&nbsp;Students will discover a world in which interdependency, networks and economic intelligence are becoming increasingly important.&nbsp;This course is an excellent companion to the Visionary and Entrepreneurial Thinking course (53-456-03).
