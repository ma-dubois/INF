TitreCours: Cash Management
DescriptionCours: This course aims to show the importance of short term financial management for the enterprise and emphasizes the role of the corporate treasurer. It will focus on cash management, but the other constituents of working capital will also be considered.  &nbsp;  The main objectives are to enable students to:   understand the key factors affecting the optimal working capital level and composition;  determine the minimum liquidity level;  master the tools and techniques of cash management;  understand short term financing and investment techniques;  know the key factors leading to successful relationships with financial institutions;  evaluate and manage the financial risks of the firm, from the perspective of the corporate treasurer;  understand risk management tolls and be able to define a "good" hedging strategy;  establish a credit policy for clients and suppliers.
