TitreCours: Selling and Negotiating
DescriptionCours: This course targets two critical skills for every Sales Representative and Business Development Manager, Selling and Negotiating in a Business to Business environment.  Complex situations where the stakes are high and where the objectives are not only to sell but to build a long term relationship with the customer will be privileged throughout the course.  The main objective will remain to learn what has to be done and how it should be completed at each selling and negotiating step in order to maximize the probability of success of each situation.
