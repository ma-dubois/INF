TitreCours: History of the English Language
DescriptionCours: This course aims to familiarize students with the important changes and stages in the history of English from its roots to the present. Topics discussed include: selected aspects of phonological, morphological, syntactic and lexical development; influence of other languages on the history of English; the development of the spelling system; ongoing change in English; sociohistorical aspects of the development of the English language; English varieties in Canada.
