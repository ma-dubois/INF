TitreCours: Financial Project Evaluation and Financing Techniques
DescriptionCours: The main objective of the course consists in presenting principles and practice of Project Finance &amp; Financial Management applied to the energy sector, with a strong accent on the understanding on the valuation, the structuring and the financing of a project in the context of risk and uncertanty.  &nbsp;  Therefore, after reviewing basic concepts, we will see how to measure a project's profitability using appropriate versions and special cases of the Net Present Value (NPV) rule.&nbsp; Since the NPV is based on forecasted cash flows, risk analysis techniques are needed to enhance the project's profitability estimation.
