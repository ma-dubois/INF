TitreCours: IT Project Management
DescriptionCours: Provide participants with a broad knowledge of the tools and methods used to manage information technology projects. Permit participants to gain hands on experience with project management tools. Use these tools to measure and manage IT project performance and analyse and manage IT project risk. At the end of the course the participant will have the knowledge and tools required to plan and implement an IT project. Participants will also have the fundamental knowledge to continue toward professional certification in IT project management.
