TitreCours: Information Technologies
DescriptionCours: Information systems constitute an important change lever for reinventing the structure, operation and management of organizations. Managers must be able to integrate IS into their management plan. The questions that must be answered to leverage systems cannot be answered by technical specialists alone. Although it may easier to let technical staff make these decisions and complain later, it is not a particularly effective means of success. The objectives of the course are therefore to:   Identify key IS decisions and understand what they imply;  Understand various means of organising the authority to make these decisions and the potential consequences of each  Become familiar with how other firms have organised to make these decisions.  At the end of the course the participant should have internalized a framework for analyzing the way IS is managed in a company and should be able to use this framework to structure a discussion with senior executives of the company
