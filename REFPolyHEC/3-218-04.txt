TitreCours: Financial Management
DescriptionCours: This course is devoted to capital budgeting and financing decisions of non-financial corporations. The four building blocks are:  &nbsp;   Investment decisions and valuation  Cost of capital  Capital structure and dividend policy  Options, futures and corporate finance  &nbsp;  The prerequisites for this course is&nbsp; Finance (2-200-96) - or&nbsp;the English equivalent's Basic Corporate Finance (2-208-97). This course will provide a deeper understanding of these topics. We will study the theoretical models and discuss their application through numerous case studies.
