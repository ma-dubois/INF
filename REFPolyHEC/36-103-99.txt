TitreCours: Business Communications - Level 3
DescriptionCours: The aim of this course is to give students at the HEC whose proficiency level is still at the intermediate level in English exposure to the practicalities of English use in today's business arena. The objective of Level 3 is to enable you to use English in practical situations such as brief presentations and case studies with confidence, and to introduce you to skills and knowledge that will assist you in your future studies and careers.
