TitreCours: CO-ADMI 840X Financial Theory and Corporate Policy Decisions
DescriptionCours: The coverage is organized around four fundamental themes in finance; namely, the neoclassical paradigm of a firm's decisions and its breakdown, information asymmetry and signaling, moral hazard and agency and corporate control. Within this broad framework, we will discuss theoretical and empirical issues relating to corporate financing decisions, capital acquisition process, corporate governance and compensation design, and mergers and acquisitions. Issues of risk management and corporate hedging will be taken up depending on the availability of time.
