TitreCours: CO-ADMI 840J Derivatives Pricing I
DescriptionCours: This course is addressed to students who have an interest in doing research work on the general topic of option pricing. It provides an advanced coverage of the general theory of derivatives pricing, and an examination of some special topics within option pricing and financial engineering that can stimulate the selection of research topics for advanced degrees in finance and mathematics. It focuses primarily on theory, but it also includes some special topics and applications. It can be extended or modified to accommodate the interests of the students taking the course, who will be encouraged to bring to the class the problems they are concerned with.  &nbsp;  &nbsp; Pour consulter l'horaire des cours, veuillez cliquer ici &nbsp;
